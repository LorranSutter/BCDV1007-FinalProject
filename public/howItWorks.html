Private and Open

Along with platform owner, each gamer with their unique Gamer ID will be able to join, read and write.

On the other hand, there will be other outside users on the platform that will be able to follow the transactions in real time and see what is going on in the open asset markets.

Proof of Elapsed Time

Proof of Elapsed Time (PoET), what is a variation of the classic Proof of Work (PoW) consensus, but it aims to eliminate the wasteful energy consumption.

It creates a similar lottery-base system to build a new block, where each node generates a random wait time and goes to sleep until the first that wake up commits the new block and broadcast the information to the network.

Hyperledger Sawtooth

Hyperledger Sawtooth has a modular architecture enabling enterprises and consortia to make policy decisions.

It provides a good range of features:

  • Dynamic consensus change consensus mechanism any time
  • Parallel transactions reduce the amount of time taken to process transactions in the system
  • Transaction receipt improve user experience with information to the client
